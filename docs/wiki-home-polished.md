# Durti-Ryce Fan Connect Wiki

Welcome to the official wiki for **Durti-Ryce Fan Connect**, a mobile-first Expo and React Native app built to give supporters one home for the full artist experience.

This wiki is the quick-reference space for project goals, app structure, development setup, backend planning, and release notes as the platform grows.

## What The App Does

Durti-Ryce Fan Connect brings together the main parts of the fan experience in one place:

- upcoming shows and ticket links
- music and video access
- merchandise browsing
- artist profiles
- fan club entry points
- newsletter signup
- social, booking, and contact links

## Current App Experience

The app currently includes these main sections:

- **Home** for the branded hero experience, featured content, and newsletter signup
- **Shows** for live dates, venues, ticket links, and map links
- **Bookings** for performance and booking-related access
- **Merch** for product browsing, cart behavior, and checkout handoff
- **Artists** for featured band member profiles
- **More** for fan club, contact, and supporting links

## Tech Stack

- **Expo**
- **React Native**
- **JavaScript**
- **Supabase** for the planned shared merch backend
- **Stripe** for planned checkout support
- **Printful** for planned fulfillment support

## Merch Status

The current merch flow supports:

- featured product browsing
- local in-app cart behavior
- quantity and total review
- handoff to the live Durti-Ryce store for final checkout

The project also includes starter backend planning files for a future shared merch system across app and web.
